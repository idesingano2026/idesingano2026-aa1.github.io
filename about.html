DeBlanca's Story

DeBlanca was founded on November 28, 2022 by a Pisay iska trying to finish her assessments. Being a subscriber of the academia aesthetic, she had an idea of a cafe/restaurant: but this time, instead of something merged with a bookstore, it was a cafe/restaurant with a big platform for people to dance on. She has a notion that bringing back simpler interactions make people closer (and another notion that her crush will share a milkshake with her:)
